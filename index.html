Instructions

1. Enter your work hours: Fill in the start and end times for each day, excluding the 45-minute break.

2. Set your hourly rate: Enter your hourly rate for each day. The rate will automatically set to £17.5 for weekends (Saturday and Sunday).

3. Calculate Total Hours and Salary: Click the "Calculate All" button to calculate your total hours and salary.

4. Night Out: You can enter the number of "Night Out" days and the rate per night to calculate your additional earnings.

5. Save and Load Data: You can save your data locally in your browser or load previously saved data to resume working.

6. Download Data: You can download your data as a .txt file to keep a record of your hours and salary.

For best view on mobile devices, rotate your screen horizontally.
